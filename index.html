Our Mission

✦✦✦

To empower families by bridging the caregiving gap for elderly and vulnerable individuals through accessible, community-based connections to trained caregivers. Our platform supports informal caregivers by alleviating their burden and ensuring every person in need receives the specialized, compassionate care they deserve.

Our Vision

✦✦✦

We aim to be New York City’s premier digital hub for caregiving coordination, where families can seamlessly connect with trusted care professionals, enhancing quality of life for individuals facing challenges with daily living due to age, injury, or chronic health conditions. We envision a future where no caregiver is overwhelmed, and no individual is left without the support they need to live with dignity.
