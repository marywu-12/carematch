Our Team

✦✦✦

Our team consists of 7 well-versed public health professionals. With experiences across pharmaceuticals, government, non-profit, medical practice sector, global health initiatives, and tech, we aim to develop smart, applicable solutions to empower underserved communities in need.

Cindy Lu

Project Director · Internal/External Liaison

Cindy is an MPH candidate in Public Health Policy, specializing in policy research. As a current employee at New York City DOHMH, she works on expanding health insurance access to marginalized communities in NYC. She has experience with working with real-time data with Broadstreet institute, policy research at the E2GI lab, and working on scalable health solutions at the AGPHI lab.

Rhea Nakra

Project Manager · Editor

Rhea Nakra is an MPH candidate in Health Policy and Management, trained as a physician with experience in medical research, healthcare policy, and global health initiatives. She has led the COVID-19 contact tracing team and her 3 years experience as a Junior Doctor in Surgical Pathology enhanced her clinical expertise and analytic skills. As a Graduate Research Assistant, she is collaborating with the United Nations Habitat HER City project to design a digital platform to empower young girls in participatory urban planning.

Vasiliki Glekas

Facilitator · Presenter

Vasiliki Glekas has had 7 years clinical experience in community pharmacy, in addition to her doctoral coursework experience in a Doctor of Pharmacy program. Beyond the classroom, Ms. Glekas has served as treasurer for a global non-profit organization for 2022-2023, and is currently conducting research by collecting survey information to create an evidence based intervention in the Undergraduate NYU population.

Megana Daliparthy

Writer · Process Consultant

Megana is currently working as research assistant at the NYU Public Health Policy Lab, analyzing the effects of firearm restrictions in establishments that sell alcohol. She has experience working as Behavior Technician implementing behavioral plans for diverse students with Autism Spectrum Disorder, tailoring interventions to meet individual needs.

Christina Joseph

Ideator · Innovator

Christina Joseph is a researcher with experience in public health policy, urban health, and healthcare management. As a Volunteer Coordinator at Caregiver Sathi, a non-profit, she led 400 volunteers, managed databases, and raised awareness about caregiver challenges.

Sage Latella

Researcher · Data Analyst

Sage Latella is an Outreach Coordinator focused on health promotion and community outreach. She has research experience in infectious endocarditis treatment (2022-2025) and expertise in literature/scoping reviews. Sage has mentored women transitioning from foster care, volunteered with youth programs, and demonstrated leadership as a student supervisor.
